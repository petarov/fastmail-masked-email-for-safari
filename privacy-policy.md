# Masked Email for Safari — Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how Masked Email for Safari uses and safeguards personal data provided by users or generated through the app.

## Core Principles

- Your data is used exactly as you expect.
- Your data is not shared with anyone, at any time.
- Your data does not leave your device without your explicit consent.

## Data Collection

Masked Email for Safari stores a Fastmail API token provided by you. This token is kept locally on your device in a shared app group container and is used solely to communicate with the Fastmail JMAP API (`api.fastmail.com`) on your behalf.

The app does not collect analytics, usage data, or any other personal information.

## Third-Party Services

The app communicates exclusively with the **Fastmail JMAP API** (`https://api.fastmail.com`) to:

- Authenticate your session using your API token.
- Fetch your existing masked email addresses.
- Create new masked email addresses.
- Update the state of existing masked email addresses (e.g. disable).

No data is sent to any other third-party service. The app does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or tracking of any kind.

## Data Storage

All data — including your API token and cached email entries — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is stored on external servers controlled by the developer.

## Data Management

You can remove your API token at any time through the app's Settings tab. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any information with anyone, at any time.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.

## Policy Status

Effective March 23, 2026.
